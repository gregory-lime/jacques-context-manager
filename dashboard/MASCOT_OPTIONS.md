## Rendering Notes

- `░` `▒` `▓` `█` = gradient shading (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- `º` = masculine ordinal (better eye than `°`)
- `●` = black circle (bold eye)
- `◉` = fisheye (glasses effect)
- `‿` = inverted arc (smile)
- `─` = box drawing horizontal
- `╰` `╯` = rounded corners
- `▖` = quadrant lower left (pipe)
- `∼` = tilde (wispy hair texture)

Colors:
- Hair: `white` or `gray`
- Face: `#FF6600` (orange)
- Pipe: `gray` (for contrast) or `#FF6600` (match face)
